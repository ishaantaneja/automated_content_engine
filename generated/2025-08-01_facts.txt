 1. In the heart of Siberia, a town called Norilsk houses the world's northernmost brewery, producing beer with an alcohol content lower than average due to the extreme cold - yet the locals drink it heated, creating a potent concoction known as "Gorilla."

2. The Eiffel Tower was originally designed to be a temporary structure for a World's Fair. But after the event ended, it was repurposed into a telecommunications antenna, saving it from demolition. This iron giant has stood since 1889, making it older than the infamous Twin Towers in New York City.
